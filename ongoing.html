Indoor Navigation

Independent mobility and navigation is a well-highlighted problem by blind and visually impaired (BVI) population. Visiting new places independently is a more chaotic and challenging experience due to poor access to navigational information,less on-ground visual and non-visual information cues and indoor structural complexity. ... There has been tremendous improvement in accessible outdoor navigation support due to ready availability of GPS devices (mobile phones) combined with solutions like Google Map, Open Street Maps (OSM), Foursquare, etc. It is enabling even for first-time visitors to navigate easily while either walking or driving. Before the advent of these technologies, because of weak signage and non-standard street numbering, even for sighted users, unassisted navigation has been a major challenge in urban centers in India and other similar developing countries.
